About Green Haven

We're a local, family-run cleaning company built on trust, attention to detail, and a genuine passion for creating healthier spaces.

Our Story

Born in Surrey, Built for the Lower Mainland

Green Haven was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves a clean, healthy space — and a cleaning company they can actually trust.

What started as a small residential cleaning operation in Surrey quickly grew as word spread about our reliability, attention to detail, and eco-conscious approach. Today, we serve hundreds of clients across the Lower Mainland, from cozy condos in Burnaby to busy offices in Vancouver and Airbnbs throughout the region.

We're proud to be locally owned and operated. Every team member is background-checked, trained to our standards, and shares our commitment to doing the job right — every time, without exception.
