Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the questions we get asked most often.

How do I get a quote?

Fill out the estimate form above or call us directly at (604) 375-9391. We'll follow up within 24 hours with a personalized, no-obligation quote.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all. Many of our clients give us a key or entry code. All team members are background-checked and fully insured for your peace of mind.

What products do you use?

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic, biodegradable cleaning products that are safe for children, pets, and the environment — without compromising on results.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Lower Mainland including Surrey, Burnaby, Vancouver, Richmond, Langley, Coquitlam, Delta, and Abbotsford. Not sure if we cover your area? Just ask!

What if I'm not satisfied with the clean?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If anything doesn't meet your expectations, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to make it right at no additional charge.
